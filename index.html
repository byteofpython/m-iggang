About

müßiggang revisited is a platform exploring the tension between serendipity and control in the digital age. The German term "müßiggang" refers to idleness or leisure that allows for contemplation and discovery.

Through essays, research, and critical analysis, we examine how algorithmic systems shape our experiences online and how we might reclaim spaces for chance encounters and unexpected discoveries.
